Mobile App Heroes

Clash Royale

#1 Free App - Google Play & iTunes Editor's Choice (2016)

As of March 2016, Clash Royale reigns as the top grossing and top free app at the Google Play Store and has been awarded Editor's Choice by the iTunes Store. As a result it is also the #1 App in the Google Play Games Category. The card-based battle game has reached over 10 million installs from Google Play alone and that number continues to grow. It was developed by the same team that created Clash of Clans

Google Chrome Browser

#1 Most Downloaded App of All Time (List 25 – 2015)

According to List 25, the Chrome Browser for Android was 2015's most downloaded app of all time. It has broken 1 billion installs on Google Play and the number is quickly growing. While it is available for Apple Devices, it tries its best to compete with Apple's Safari mobile browser.
